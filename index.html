About Ignite 4.0

Ignite is one of the iconic annual flagship events conducted by Women in Engineering (WIE) wing of the IEEE Travancore Hub. It is a renowned event among the IEEE Kerala Section activities, as it focuses purely on the development female student members. Ignite is the opportunity provided by IEEE Travancore Hub to allow their student members to communicate, and interact with other participants, speakers and volunteers.

Ignite introduces students to the variety of opportunities provided by IEEE, in terms of technical skills. The students receive valuable experience and advice from distinguished guests and speakers, who are also women that are accomplished in their respective careers. Along with talks, a series of workshops are also conducted which allows students to improve upon their technical skills, which in turn boosts their self-confidence and thinking abilities. The end goal of Ignite is to provide its student members the chance to interact with like-minded and powerful women who are successful, thereby inspiring and motivating students to follow their lead.

Ignite is the annual WIE congress of IEEE Travancore Hub. It is to be conducted as a two day event consisting of women centric events, both technical and general.
